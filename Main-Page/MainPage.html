About the web application

This web application is a Singapore Hawker Centre Management System designed to support customers, stall owners, operators, and NEA officers through a single digital platform. The application allows customers to browse food stalls, view menus, place orders, make payments, and provide feedback such as ratings, comments, likes, and complaints. Stall owners and operators can manage menus, track orders, monitor stall performance, and run promotions, while NEA officers can record inspections, hygiene scores, and issue hygiene grades for regulatory compliance. By integrating ordering, customer engagement, and hygiene monitoring features, the system helps improve operational efficiency, food safety standards, and overall customer experience at hawker centres.
